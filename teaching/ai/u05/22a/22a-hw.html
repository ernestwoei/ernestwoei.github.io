Math 22A Homework

Instruction:

Every week several homework problems are assigned. You are supposed to solve them all, because the exams (midterms and the final) are based largely on the homework assignments. There will be problems marked as Submission Problems. They are due in class every Wednesday.
  • Print clearly (1) your full name; (2) the course number (Math 22A); and (3) the due date; at the top right corner of the front page of your homework paper.
  • Please staple your homework if you use more than one sheet of paper.
  • Submit your answer to the homework problems marked submission problems.
  • The graded homework papers will be returned in the Homework Boxes at the basement of Wellman or in class.
